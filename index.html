NICE TO MEET YOU, Let's get to know each other better.

André Costa is a tattoo artist with experience since 2021. Initially, he explored a diversity of styles, tattooing close friends.

His passion for the old school traditional European style blossomed after visits to specialized studios and personal experiences with tattoo artists of that genre.

Today, André stands out for his ability to capture the essence of old school traditional European, offering meticulous and authentic work that reflects his dedication and talent in the art of tattooing.
